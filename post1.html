Piano Time - Für Elise the ears

18.12.2022 - Author: Patrick

Our new piano has arrived. After a break of nearly 12 years my wife and I bought a new piano. Well - a kind of piano. We bought a digital piano for several reasons

  • They sound pretty well. Not as good as a real one, but if you compare it to older digital pianos you have to admit that they have come a long way.
  • They are light compared to a real piano. With only 50 kg they are much lighter than a piano which can weight up to 300 kg.
  • Headphone support. Even if the sound of piano is very nice, the practice session can sound quite monotonous. So it is great to have the ability to put some headphones in it. That way your neighbors don't need to listen to your playing.

The piano of choice

We chose a Kawai CN-29 because it has a good sound for a very good price. The hardest decision came down to the choice of color. We took the white one. :)

Conclusion

We are very happy with our choice and we are practicing every day to get better. Well almost every day... :)
